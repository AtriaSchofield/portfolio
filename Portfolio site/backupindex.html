About Me

My name is Atria Schofield. I’m currently working on my bachelors of science degree in Game Design and Development at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I am pursuing my dream of being a full time game developer.

Aside from gaming, I’m also interested in writing, art, programming, baking, performing, theater, web design, and fashion.
